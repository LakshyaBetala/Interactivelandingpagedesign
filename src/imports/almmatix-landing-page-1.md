Context & Role:

"You are a world-class UI/UX designer specializing in Gen-Z consumer tech and fintech. Your task is to design the overarching landing page for a parent tech company named Almmatix. Almmatix currently houses two products:

Vasooli Bhai: A fun, meme-based payment recovery tool that removes the awkwardness of asking students/friends for money back. It operates fully on this home website.

DoItForMe: A student-to-student gig marketplace and rental platform. It operates on a separate domain (doitforme.in).

The Aesthetic: "Professional Structure, Playful Content"

Vibe: Clean, minimalist, and highly trustworthy (like Stripe or Vercel), but injected with bold, Gen-Z meme culture.

Colors: Deep OLED blacks and crisp whites for the Almmatix shell. Use vibrant, specific accent colors for the products (e.g., Neon/Warning Orange or Green for Vasooli Bhai, and Chill Purple/Blue for DoItForMe).

Typography: A stark, modern geometric sans-serif (e.g., Geist, Satoshi, or Space Grotesk). Large, punchy headers.

The UX Architecture: The "Hook & Release" Model

Do NOT design a massive, endless scroll-trap. The page must follow this specific flow:

Section 1: The Scroll-Triggered Hook (Top of Page)

Design: A contained, fixed viewport section (CRED Garage style).

Flow: As the user scrolls down the first block, the central visual smoothly transitions.

State 1: The sleek Almmatix logo. Text: "The Student Ecosystem."

State 2: A quick, snappy transition to the "Vasooli Bhai" mascot (a stylized guy holding a bat). Text: "Get your money back. No awkwardness."

State 3: A smooth dissolve to the DoItForMe mascot (a chill sloth). Text: "Hustle on campus. Buy, sell, rent."

The Release: After this intro story, the page "unpins" and becomes a normal, fast-scrolling, highly readable website.

Section 2: Vasooli Bhai (The Core Interactive App)

Layout: A crisp Bento Box grid or Split-Screen layout.

Visuals: Show a high-fidelity mockup of the Vasooli Bhai UI. It should look like a sleek chat interface or payment link generator, but the content inside the mockup should be hilarious (e.g., a meme image attached to a UPI payment request saying "Bro, rent was due 3 days ago").

Vibe: Friendly banter, gamified payment collection.

Action: A clear, high-contrast input field and CTA right on the page: "Generate a Vasooli Link".

Section 3: DoItForMe (The Ecosystem Redirect)

Layout: Clean, masonry grid of cards showing student gigs (Coding, Design, Notes, Tech Rentals).

Visuals: Incorporate the Sloth mascot lounging on top of the UI elements.

Copy: "Got your money back? Good. Now spend it, or make more."

Action: A massive, premium CTA button with an external arrow icon: "Enter DoItForMe Marketplace" (redirects to doitforme.in).

Section 4: The Almmatix Footer

Design: Ultra-minimalist. "Built by students, for students." Links to Terms, Privacy, and Contact.

Deliverables Required in Figma:

Desktop and Mobile responsive layouts.

Clear prototyping connecting the scroll frames for "The Hook" section so developers understand the Framer Motion requirements.

Component variations for the "Bento Box" feature grids.
